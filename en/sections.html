Panel Discussions / Sections

1. Strategic Directions and Factors of Human Capital Formation

  • Educational and scientific priorities for the socio-economic revival of Ukraine
  • New forms of learning and scientific research in the context of digital transformation
  • Artificial intelligence in science and education
Main organizer: National Academy of Educational Sciences of Ukraine, Ukrainian Union of Industrialists and Entrepreneurs.

2. Science as a Driver of Post-War Revival and Innovation

  • The role of fundamental research in creating new technologies and materials
  • State support and international cooperation in the field of fundamental science
  • Challenges and opportunities for the development of fundamental sciences in the context of the country's reconstruction
Main organizer: National Academy of Sciences of Ukraine.

3. Artificial Intelligence: A Mind That Changes the World

  • Creating innovative university infrastructure: legal and organizational challenges.
  • (within the panel’s work, an exhibition of innovative startup projects by Ukrainian educators and scholars is also planned)
Main organizer: Ukrainian Peace Council, Minor Academy of Sciences of Ukraine

4. European Vector of the Development of Ukrainian Science and Education

  • Harmonization of Ukrainian science and education with European standards
  • Adaptation of European practices for training specialists for the new economy
Main organizer: Taras Shevchenko National University of Kyiv.

5. Lifelong Learning: Economic, Social-Labour, and Institutional Challenges

  • Higher education within the coordinates of the new digital economy.
  • Higher and platform-based education for the “silver economy”.
  • Professional and social adaptation in veteran development policy.
  • Strategies for developing higher education in the context of the emergence of “Industry 5.0” and “Work 5.0”.
Main organizer: Kyiv National Economic University named after Vadym Hetman, International Scientific and Technical University named after Academician Yuriy Bugay

6. Training a New Generation of Teachers and Educators

  • Competencies of the modern teacher and educator: innovation, critical thinking, digital literacy
  • Education as a means of forming national identity and restoring social unity
Main organizer: Ukrainian State University named after Mykhailo Drahomanov.

7. Youth – a Driver of the Development of Science and Education

  • Youth participation in reintegration processes
  • The issue of returning young people to Ukraine
Main organizer: Youth Wing of the Ukrainian Peace Council, Ukrainian Alliance

8. International Cooperation: Education, Science, Innovation in Ukraine

Main organizer: General Directorate for Foreign Missions Services, Ukrainian Association of Foreign Policy
